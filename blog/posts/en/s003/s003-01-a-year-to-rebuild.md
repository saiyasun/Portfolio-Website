I am not starting from zero.

I already lift. I walk a lot. I have built muscle, improved my body, and learned enough to know that fitness is not some mystery reserved for other people.

But I also know I am not where I want to be.

There is a difference between being in decent shape and having the body I actually want to live in. I want more strength, better mobility, better conditioning, better movement quality, and a physique that reflects a deeper level of discipline.

So I am giving myself a year.

Not to become perfect. Not to chase some impossible transformation. But to rebuild with intention and see how far I can go when I stop treating fitness like something I only improve in pieces.

## Where I Am Now

Talk about current strengths and weaknesses. You are not unfit, but you have gaps.

## Why a Year?

Explain that a year is long enough to make real changes without crash-diet / quick-fix thinking.

## What I Want To Build

Strength, mobility, physique, conditioning, consistency, recovery.

## How I Plan To Approach It

Training, nutrition, mobility work, tracking progress, check-ins, honest updates.

## Goal

A body that is strong, mobile, capable, and actually feels good to live in.
