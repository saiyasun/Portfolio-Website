About

I’m Asiah Crutchfield (孫賽亞) — a computer science student passionate about exploring the intersection of learning, technology, and creativity. I create digital projects and videos that experiment with new ways of understanding the world and sharing knowledge.

Originally from the U.S., I moved to Taiwan to pursue my degree in computer science and to immerse myself in a different culture. This leap has challenged me, but it’s also given me invaluable experiences and insights. One of my current focuses is developing an innovative language-learning project designed to change how people approach foreign languages.

Outside of school and programming, I’m dedicated to strength training and continually testing my limits in the gym. I’m also studying Mandarin Chinese, working toward professional proficiency, and love exploring Taiwan’s culture, people, and places.
